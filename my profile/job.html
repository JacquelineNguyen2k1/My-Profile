Ideal Job

DESCRIPTION

This job requires writing technical articles, documentation and manuals to provide users with information about new products and updates from the company. A technical writer has to work with a team comprised of managers, editors, developers and experts to finish the product. I would love to collaborate with other people in the field in order to help the public understand more about IT and its usefulness in every aspect of our life.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Prior working experience and knowledge in the field
  • Ability to convey complexed technical ideas to the masses in the form of brief yet understandable reading materials
  • Passion
  • Good command of American English
  • Familiarity with using one or some design softwares
  • Bachelor degree in computer science, computer engineering, or a related field
