Personal Profile

My Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) results

Meaning

According to these results, I am a sympathetic kind of person with "an inborn sense of idealism and morality". Helping others is my purpose in life. When some people are troubled, instead of thinking about their suffering, I will start thinking up ways to make their situation better. However, this optimism does not often go well with practicality and lead to negative outcomes. I might overlook important problems, focus on the bright side and get upset when things are unexpected. Another trait of mine is that I am not deeply influenced by opinions of others and choose to go my own way, which sometimes can be detrimental.

In the workplace, I am likely to attract many colleagues and act as a mediator to defuse the situation. However, my inner circle of friends consists of only a few trusted people. Advocates like me are still introverts and individualists, so although I enjoy company, I will prefer solitude from time to time. I am also prone to putting more importance on teamwork over overall performance and being a pushover. Stress does not strlongly affect me, which is a bonus.

Impact on teamwork

I am likely to agree with the majority on a subject and work as an anchor of the team. Whenever there appears hostility, I will try to alleviate the problem. When a team member has troubles, I may try to help them with their work or personal issues. It might be hard work juggling between assignments and drama among the team, but I may not be affected much and just keep going.

Food for thought

When assembling a team, my prority is who my team members are. I will prefer working with people I trust, because then I will not waste time obssessing over their work progress and efficiency. The members should be familiar and compatible with each other to avoid unnecessary drama. I prefer delegating tasks so that each member do their own thing, which will give me some space to work individually. I would like to be the one to vet the final product and make sure everyone is happy with it.

All in all, teamwork is important.

My creativity test results

Meaning

I am more creative than normal standards by only a margin. Curiosity is my most valuable creativity aspect. My ability to connect seemingly unrelated concepts are much higher than normal. I am more persistent in finding new solutions to problems and more prone to pushing boundaries. I am less likely to accept and work with contradictory statements, and I am terrible at multitasking and handling large amounts of raw data.

Impact on teamwork

I am likely to be the one to propose ideas and solutions, though unable to sort them properly. I will be difficult to reason with if there is no concrete proof. Additionally, I may be a slow worker, seeing that I can only work one thing at a time, which may hinder the team's progress .

Food for thought

I figure I need team members who are logical, first of all. They should be open-minded and tolerant of unconventional ideas. There should be someone who is good at compiling data and someone else who can help me work faster.
